For Large-Screen(Desktop)

1.This Is An Simple Front-End Web Design Featuring Heros Images on The Screen.

2.At the Top Of The Site Featuring a Header With an Background-Image and Featuring Naveen Kumar at The left Side And The Works , About At The Right Side of The Header.

3.The Main Content Has Images as an 4*4 Grid Layout with an Animation Background. When We Hover Over the Images We can get the Details like (Name , Age) on the Image With an Blur Background Effect.

4.When You Click on the Naveen Kumar You Will Gets Displayed the Main Content and If You Click The Works Element You Will Still Gets The Same Main Content, and if You Click About You Will Get The Details Of The Developer Of This Website.

1. When You Click On Naveen Kumar

2. When You Click On Works

3. When You Click On About

Apart from this,

1.Plane Animation At The Top Of Main Content Moving From Top-Left to Top-Right.

2.When We Hover Over The Plane, The Plane Gets Stopped Until You Move to the Next Element.

3.In This Site, You See Two Different Balls Moving On Two Different Directions:

  • A White Ball Moving From Top-Left to Bottom-Left At Each Step When It Touches (Left/Right) Side its Colour Gets Changed and Size gets Increased.
  • A Red Ball Moving From Bottom-Right To Top-Right

4.When You Hover Over The First Ball, The Ball Gets Stopped till You Move Your Mouse And When You Click On the Ball An Message Will Be Shown "You Caught The Ball" On The Middle Of The Header and get Disappeared within 1s And The Score Will be Calculated as 1.At Each Time When You Click On The 1st Ball Your Score Gets Incremented by 1(Refer To The Console).

5.When You Accidentally Click On The 2nd Ball(Red Ball), Your Total Score Will be Displayed On The Screen With an Alert Message,your Score Will gets Reset and The Page Gets Newly Reloaded Within 2s.

1. A Game Based Approach(Slow Mode).

2. A Game Based Approach(Fast Mode).

For Smaller-Screens(Mobile-Phones)

1.This Is An Simple Front-End Web Design Featuring Heros Images on The Screen.

2.At the Top Of The Site Featuring a Header With an Background-Image and Featuring Naveen Kumar at The left Side And The Menu Button At The Right Side of The Header.

3.The Main Content Has Images With Layout 1*1 One by One When You Hover You Will Get the Details like (Name,Age).

4.When You Click On The Menu Button An Side Bar Will Be Opened With Elements like Works,About,Program Structure,Documentation.

5.Works,About Elements Works As like the Desktop Screen

6.When You Click Program Structure In The Side-bar a Structure Page Will be Opened With the Structure of The Program

7.When You Click On The Documentation Element an Documentation Page Will Be Opened With Details About the Web-Page

NOTE:

1.The Program Structure and Documentation Page Will Be Only Available In Mobile-Screens.

2.If You Rotate The Screen in Mobile Phone (Landscape-Mode) it is Considered as Large Screen or Medium Screen can't be Considered as Small Screen.
